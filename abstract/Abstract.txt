This Program is an implementation to make a Hardware-Software Interface in order to facilitate the simulation of external hardwares with MATLAB Simulink.

The peripherals which were considered are as follows:

1 - Analog to Digital Converters
2 - Digital Inputs
3 - Digital Outputs
4 - Encoder Read
5 - PWM DC Motor Drivers
6 - PWM Write
7 - Ethernet TCP/IP Connection
8 - USB Interface
